Biography

I'm currently working on GCP network infrastructure for Google. My research spans broadly in Computer Networks. I am particularly interested in Big Data, Network Security and Privacy, Cyber-physical Systems, RFID and Internet of Things.

I received my PhD in Computer Science from University of Florida in 2017 under the supervision of Prof. Shigang Chen. I received my B.E. degree in Electronic Engineering from University of Science and Technology of China in 2013.
